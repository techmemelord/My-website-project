Just what is Garfield???

Garfield is a fat, lazy, lasagana-loving cat that was created in 1978 by a cartoonist known as Jim Davis. His design may have changed over the years, but he still keeps his monday-hating attitude more alive than ever before! He is recognized as one of the most widely syndicated comic strips in the world, appearing in over 2,000 newspapers and having a huge fanbase and multiple movies!

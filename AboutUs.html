What is the Garfield Museum?

The Garfield Museum is a madeup museum in a website made for a school project. This was made by someone who loves Garfield and wanted to share their love for the comic strip with others. This website is not affiliated with Jim Davis or Paws, Inc. in any way or form.

What was the inspiration for this website?

Part of the inspiration for this website comes from the Garfield trail in Grant County, Indiana. The trail is a series of statues and murals that celebrate the comic strip and its creator, Jim Davis. The trail is located in Indiana in the towns of Fairmont, Muncie, Gas City, Marion and other small towns and cities. The trail is a fun way to explore the area and learn a bit more about Garfield and the comic's origins.

Why was this website made?

This website was used to imagine what a true Garfield muesum would be like and what it would have. Since there is no museums at all dedicating to Garfield, I took the opportunity to create one myself. This website is a fun way to explore the world of Garfield and learn more about the comic strip and its characters.
I hope you enjoy exploring the website and learning more about Garfield!
